Proposition 1.1.32.

If \(V\) is a vector space whose dimension \(\dim V = n\) and \(\vvec_1,\vvec_2,\ldots,\vvec_n\) is a linearly independent set in \(V\text{,}\) then \(\vvec_1,\vvec_2,\ldots,\vvec_n\) also spans \(V\) and is therefore a basis of \(V\text{.}\)
